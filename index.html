Gentle Reminders

Don't delay going to the hospital if you have any of these serious symptoms.
  • Difficulty in breathing
  • Constant pain or pressure in your chest
  • Bluish lips or face
  • Sudden confusion
  • Feeling faint or on the verge of a stroke.
