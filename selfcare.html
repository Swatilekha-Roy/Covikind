How to Take Care of Yourself?

  • Take some time off from your screens.
  • Appreciate your caregivers and the community that is helping you out.
  • Limit yourself from watching too much news, especially related to the pandemic.
  • Listen to music or read lighthearted books.
  • Indulge yourself in hobbies.
  • Try meditating for a few minutes everyday.
  • Stay calm and keep yourself hydrated.
  • Set reminders for timing food breaks, meds and other necessities so that you can avoid anxiety.
